The Bellcycle is like no other bike

The Bellcycle features front wheel drive for an ultra compact bicycle. The riding position is upright and relaxed with your hands naturally by your side. The Bellcycle comes in a kit of 100+ parts which you assemble yourself. And because you assembled it you can remake it to suit your needs from a cargo bike to electric bike.
